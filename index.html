About us

Digital Valley-is an international summer camp focused on information technologies organized jointly with the Food and Agriculture Organization of the United Nations(FAO) The United Nations Population Fund(UNFPA).Digital City technology park and Andijon region admistration. The main goal of the “Digital Valley”camp is to develop coopertion and friendship between the youth of the neighboring republics.The camp is aimed to create a foundation for the development of join projects,increase the ICT potential of the Fergana Valley and turn the Andijan region into an IT hub of the Republic of Uzbekistan. The camps is planned to be organized in the city of Khanabad,which is considered a peaceful and picturesque corner of the Andijon region During the camp participant will improve their knowledge and skills with the help of international trainers,participate in various sport events,meetings with entreprenerous who have achieved success in the IT field and enjoy unforgattable cultural evenings organized at a high level.
